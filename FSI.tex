ABSTRACT:
A staggared grid, semi-implicit, finite volume, moment of fluid (sharp
interface), adaptive mesh
algorithm is presented for computing solutions to general multiphase flows.
\linenumbers
\section{Description of the mathematics}
The combination of (a) semi-implicit compressible projection method for
simultaneously computing low and high speed flows, 
(b) sharp interface moment of fluid and continuous moment of fluid 
method for representing sharp complex multi-phase deforming interfaces, 
(c) Eulerian algorithm for simulatig fluid structure interaction
for realistic materials undergoing plastic  deformation, (d) 
parallel adaptive mesh refinement for high performance computing,
and (e) ``anecdotal'' stable and consistent numerical results,
make our method unique.
